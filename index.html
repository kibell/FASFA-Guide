Let's take a minute to see how you're feeling

To make sure you’re feeling tip-top for your first term, we’ve created a (very) brief survey to see how you’re feeling about a few things that sometimes cause students to feel nervous.

Your admission counselor will receive your answers and reach out to you with help and info if you’re not feeling super confident on anything.

Super Confident! Confident! A Bit Shaky Help!
How are you feeling about your tech requirements (having a laptop, internet connection, etc.)?
How are you feeling about being able to log in to your SNHU email, mySNHU and your Brightspace courses?
How are you feeling about obtaining and access your assigned books and course materials?
How are you feeling about knowing how much time you’ll need to spend on your coursework each week and what days and times you’ll schedule to study?
How confident are you about knowing how to contact all the SNHU support teams who are ready to help you (advisor, faculty, tutors,  mental health counselors, academic accommodations, etc.)?
How do you feel about your ability to find and complete your assignments in Brightspace?
How are you feeling about paying for college?
How much do you believe in yourself to succeed this term, and how much do you feel that you belong at SNHU? (We believe in you!)
How are you feeling overall (health, family,work, living situation)?
